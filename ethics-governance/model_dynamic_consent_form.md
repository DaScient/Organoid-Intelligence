## 1. The Nature of Organoid Intelligence
You are being asked to donate somatic cells (e.g., a skin biopsy or blood draw). These cells will be reprogrammed into induced Pluripotent Stem Cells (iPSCs) and then grown into **Cerebral Organoids** (living, 3D miniature brain tissues). 

Unlike traditional biomedical research where your tissue is studied and discarded, your cells will be integrated with computer hardware to create a biological computing system. **Your cells will be used to process information, recognize patterns, and potentially control digital or robotic environments.**

## 2. Core Disclosures
By participating, you acknowledge understanding of the following:
* [cite_start]**Duration:** Your organoids may be kept alive and utilized for computational research for months or years[cite: 54].
* **Data Generation:** Your organoid will generate vast amounts of electrophysiological and genomic data. [cite_start]This data contains an irreducible connection to your specific genetic identity[cite: 54].
* **Moral Status:** While current organoids are not believed to be conscious, they generate spontaneous neural activity. [cite_start]We cannot entirely exclude the possibility that future, highly complex organoids may develop morally relevant properties[cite: 54].
* **Commercialization:** Computations, algorithms, or products derived from your organoid's activity may be patented or commercialized by the Institution. (See Section 4 regarding benefit-sharing) [cite_start][cite: 54].

## 3. Tiered Consent Options
Organoid Intelligence requires ongoing, dynamic consent. Please indicate which applications you consent to. [cite_start]You may update these preferences at any time via the Institution's secure digital portal[cite: 54].

- [ ] **Tier 1 (Basic Research):** I consent to my organoids being used for fundamental neuroscience and computational benchmarking.
- [ ] **Tier 2 (Applied AI/Drug Discovery):** I consent to my organoids being used as biological neural network accelerators for drug screening and AI pattern recognition.
- [ ] [cite_start]**Tier 3 (Commercialization):** I consent to my organoids being used to develop commercial products, patents, or software IP[cite: 54].
- [ ] **Tier 4 (Data Sharing):** I consent to the electrophysiological outputs of my organoid being shared in open-source international databases.
- [ ] **EXCLUSION:** I specifically **DO NOT** consent to my organoids being used for [ military applications / autonomous bio-robotics / insert other ].

## 4. Withdrawal and Destruction
You have the right to withdraw from this research at any time. Upon withdrawal:
1. [cite_start]All living organoids derived from your cells currently held by the PI will be immediately destroyed via autoclaving[cite: 54].
2. You acknowledge that data *already* generated by your organoid's computations cannot be erased from completed models or published papers.
3. If your iPSC line has been shared with Tier 5 international collaborators (if opted-in), the Institution will issue a mandatory destruction order, though international enforcement cannot be guaranteed.
